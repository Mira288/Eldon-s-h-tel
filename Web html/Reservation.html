AZALAÏ HÔTEL COTONOU

Located in Cotonou, 2 km from the UNDP office, the Azalaï Hotel Cotonou has an outdoor swimming pool and offers a view of the sea. free shuttle bus and restaurant. The establishment also offers a reception open 24 hours a day and a currency exchange desk.

BENIN ROYAL HÔTEL

Located in Cotonou, the Benin Royal Hotel has an indoor swimming pool and a spa with wellness center. You will enjoy a connection Free Wi-Fi.Its air-conditioned rooms are all equipped with satellite television. Their private bathroom is equipped with a bathtub or a shower and a hairdryer. NOBILA AIRPORT HOTEL

Located in Cotonou, Nobila Airport Hotel offers a restaurant and free Wi-Fi. All accommodations include air conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar. The dining area is equipped with a refrigerator and an electric kettle. The private bathroom has a shower, bath and hairdryer.

HOTEL MAISON ROUGE COTONOU

Located in Cotonou, Hotel Maison Rouge Cotonou offers 2 outdoor pools, a restaurant and a connection Free Wi-Fi.The rooms are equipped with a minibar, air conditioning and a private bathroom with shower. The Hotel Maison Rouge Cotonou provides you with a airport shuttle, gardens, terraces, a shared lounge and a meeting room. On-site parking is free..

The hotel offers rooms for up to 2 people with a minimum of 1 adult.
